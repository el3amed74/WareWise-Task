### Checklist Views
Located in `resources/views/checklists/`

#### Available Views
- `index.blade.php`: List of all checklists
- `create.blade.php`: Form to create new checklist
- `edit.blade.php`: Form to edit existing checklist
- `show.blade.php`: Detailed view of a checklist

### Checklist Question Views
Located in `resources/views/checklist-questions/`

#### Available Views
- `index.blade.php`: List of questions for a checklist
- `create.blade.php`: Form to create new question
- `edit.blade.php`: Form to edit existing question
